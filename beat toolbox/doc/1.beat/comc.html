comc

(beat/comc.m)

Function Synopsis

r = comc (data)

Help text

 COMC   Compute coefficient of multiple correlation of a data series.
   R = COMC (DATA) returns the coefficient of multiple correlation of the 
   numbers contained in DATA.  
   DATA is assumed to be a matrix, with each column being a series of data.
